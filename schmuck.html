Hi, I'm Rohan 👋

I'm currently a second year at the University of Michigan studying computer engineering, with a bit of dedicated CS and EE sprinkled in.

When I'm not doing school related things, I'm either building things (hardware & software) or trying to give back to the community. Ideally, I'm doing both at the same time.

As part of Atlas Digital, I work on several pro-bono technical projects for companies in and around the Ann Arbor area to improve their day-to-day operations using code - everything from automating ordering ingredients and managing reservations to creating apps for students to download. And as co-director of Shift Creator Space, I help mentor Creators, helping them explore differentn passions to make their imagination a reality - whether it’s a collage of artworks or a startup. Through community events, we nurture our Creators and forge genuine relationships between each member of the community.

And when I’m trying to keep it low-key, you'll find me buried in yet another book, listening to music, or *trying* to learn how to sketch.

Feel free to check out some of my past work & definitely reach out if you want to talk more!
